Simple Process

How It Works For You

From your first enquiry to workers arriving on-site — a simple, transparent 4-step process.

1
Submit Manpower Request
Tell us your requirements — job category, number of workers, destination, and timeline. We'll confirm availability within 24 hours.
2
Candidate Shortlisting
We source, trade-test, and shortlist candidates from our database. You receive detailed CVs and can conduct video interviews if needed.
3
Documentation & Visa
We handle all medical screenings, BMET clearances, visa stamping, and pre-departure orientation on your behalf.
4
Deployment & Support
Workers arrive on-site on schedule. We remain available for ongoing support throughout the employment period.
