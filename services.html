How It Works

Our Recruitment Process

We follow a rigorous, transparent, and fully compliant 5-step process to ensure quality for employers and safety for every worker we deploy.

✓
Rigorous skill testing before shortlisting
✓
Medical screenings at government-approved centers
✓
Full BMET Smart Card & government clearance
✓
Pre-departure orientation for destination country
Register as a Candidate
1
Sourcing & Screening

We source candidates from our vast database and conduct rigorous trade testing to verify real skills — testing a welder's precision, a driver's safety knowledge, or a mason's technique firsthand.

2
Medical & Documentation

Comprehensive medical check-ups at government-approved centers, combined with full verification of legal documents including NID and Passport validity.

3
Visa & BMET Processing

We handle the entire visa stamping process and secure all necessary government clearances including the BMET Smart Card — ensuring 100% legal migration status.

4
Pre-Departure Orientation (PDO)

Workers are educated on the culture, labor laws, and safety regulations of their destination country — from KSA labor law to Japanese workplace etiquette and European safety standards.

5
Deployment & Ongoing Support

Safe travel arrangements and handover to the employer on arrival. Our support continues after deployment — ensuring smooth worker integration and addressing any issues that arise.
