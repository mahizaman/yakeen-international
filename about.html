Our Story

Why We Started

Workers in Bangladesh and surrounding regions have long faced serious challenges when trying to pursue employment abroad. These are the problems we set out to solve:

Fake job offers from unverified agents
Unclear and exploitative employment contracts
Hidden or excessive service fees with no transparency
Illegal migration processing putting workers at risk

These issues motivated us to create a transparent, trustworthy, and professional manpower agency. We work only with verified employers, ensure legal contracts, and maintain full transparency at every step — with one goal: eliminating exploitation and providing real, reliable opportunities.

How It Works

Our Recruitment Process

We follow a rigorous, transparent, and fully compliant 5-step process to ensure quality for employers and safety for every worker we deploy.

✓
Rigorous skill testing before shortlisting
✓
Medical screenings at government-approved centers
✓
Full BMET Smart Card & government clearance
✓
Pre-departure orientation for destination country
Register as a Candidate
1
Sourcing & Screening

We source candidates from our vast database and conduct rigorous trade testing to verify real skills — testing a welder's precision, a driver's safety knowledge, or a mason's technique firsthand.

2
Medical & Documentation

Comprehensive medical check-ups at government-approved centers, combined with full verification of legal documents including NID and Passport validity.

3
Visa & BMET Processing

We handle the entire visa stamping process and secure all necessary government clearances including the BMET Smart Card — ensuring 100% legal migration status.

4
Pre-Departure Orientation (PDO)

Workers are educated on the culture, labor laws, and safety regulations of their destination country — from KSA labor law to Japanese workplace etiquette and European safety standards.

5
Deployment & Ongoing Support

Safe travel arrangements and handover to the employer on arrival. Our support continues after deployment — ensuring smooth worker integration and addressing any issues that arise.

"Every worker deserves a fair chance at a better life abroad."

When I founded Yakeen International, I had one clear purpose in mind — to create a recruitment agency that workers could actually trust. Having witnessed firsthand how so many hardworking people from our communities were being deceived, exploited, and left vulnerable by fraudulent agents, I knew something had to change.

The name Yakeen (يقين) means "certainty" in Arabic — and that is exactly what we provide. Certainty that your job is real. Certainty that your contract is fair. Certainty that your visa is legal. And certainty that we will stand by you every step of the way.

We are not just a business. We are a bridge between ambition and opportunity — built on the foundation of honesty, transparency, and genuine care for the people we serve. Every file we process, every worker we deploy, every employer we partner with — it all comes back to one principle: doing things the right way.

To every worker who walks through our doors: your trust is our greatest responsibility. And to every employer who partners with us: we promise you nothing less than our full commitment to quality and compliance.

Together, let us build a future built on Yakeen — certainty, integrity, and opportunity for all.
