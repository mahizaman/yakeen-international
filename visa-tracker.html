Understanding Your Status

How the Process Works

Here's what each status in your tracking timeline means.

📋
Application Received
Your documents have been submitted to us and your file has been opened. We are reviewing your application and matching you with a verified employer.
🏥
Medical Done
Your medical examination at an approved center has been completed and results have been submitted to the employer and relevant authorities.
🛂
Visa Stamped
Your visa has been officially approved and stamped in your passport. You are now legally cleared to travel to your destination country.
✈️
Ticket Issued
Your flight ticket has been booked and issued. You will receive your travel details and departure date from our office shortly.
🌍
Deployed
Congratulations! You have successfully departed and been handed over to your employer. Your new career abroad has begun!
❓
Don't See Your Status?
If your passport number gives no result, it may not be registered yet. Please visit our office or contact us to register your application.
