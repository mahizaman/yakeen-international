Approved Testing & Medical Centres

Find official BMET-approved trade testing facilities to verify your skills, and understand the GAMCA medical clearance process for overseas deployment.

Skill Verification

Government-Approved Trade Testing Centres

Before deploying abroad, technical workers (like masons, electricians, welders, and plumbers) must have their skills assessed. The following are major BMET-approved Technical Training Centres (TTC) in Bangladesh where verification takes place.

Bangladesh-Korea Technical Training Centre (BKTTC)
Mirpur Road, Darus Salam, Dhaka
Specialties: Automotive, Electrical, Welding, CNC
Bangladesh German Technical Training Centre (BGTTC)
Mirpur-2, Dhaka
Specialties: Construction, Plumbing, Carpentry, MEP
Sheikh Fazilatunnesa Mujib Mohila TTC
Mirpur Road, Darus Salam, Dhaka
Specialties: Garments, IT, General Technical Trades
Health Clearance

GAMCA / BMET Approved Medical Centres

Important GAMCA Policy

Workers traveling to GCC countries (Saudi Arabia, UAE, Oman, Qatar, Kuwait, Bahrain) cannot choose their own medical centre. You must first generate a GAMCA slip online (via Wafid). The system will randomly assign you to one of the approved medical centres below. Yakeen International will assist you with generating this slip.

Gulshan Medicare
House 14, Road 16, Gulshan-1, Dhaka
Approved for: Saudi Arabia, UAE, Qatar
Al-Hayat Medical Centre
Purana Paltan, Dhaka
Approved for: GCC Countries (Wafid)
KSA Medical Centre
Banani, Dhaka
Approved for: Saudi Arabia, Oman, Kuwait
