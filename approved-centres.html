GAMCA / Wafid Approved Medical Centres

Important GAMCA Policy

Workers traveling to GCC countries (Saudi Arabia, UAE, Oman, Qatar, Kuwait, Bahrain) cannot manually select their medical centre. You must generate a GAMCA slip via the Wafid system, which randomly assigns you to one of the approved facilities below.
